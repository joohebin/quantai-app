Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Welcome to the QuantAI community. These guidelines outline the expected behavior for all users participating in our community features, including Trading Square, Strategy Marketplace, Signal Broadcast, Leaderboards, and Copy Trading.

Our goal is to create a respectful, transparent, and valuable environment for traders worldwide.

1. Core Principles

  • Respect: Treat all community members with dignity and respect.
  • Honesty: Share genuine insights and be transparent about your intentions.
  • Responsibility: You are accountable for the content you post and the signals you share.
  • Compliance: Follow all applicable laws and these guidelines at all times.

2. Content Standards

✅ Do:

  • Share well-reasoned market analysis and trading ideas
  • Provide constructive feedback on strategies and signals
  • Report your strategy performance honestly and accurately
  • Help other users learn by sharing your knowledge and experience
  • Use appropriate language and maintain a professional tone
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest (e.g., holding a position you're recommending)

❌ Don't:

  • Post false or misleading trading results, performance claims, or market information
  • Guarantee profits or promise specific returns
  • Share or solicit personal information (API keys, passwords, personal contact details)
  • Spam, flood, or post repetitive content
  • Promote or link to unauthorized third-party services, scams, or phishing sites
  • Engage in market manipulation, pump-and-dump schemes, or coordinated trading tactics
  • Post content that is hateful, discriminatory, threatening, or harassing
  • Share insider information or encourage illegal trading activities

3. Strategy Marketplace Rules

  • All strategies must be submitted with accurate descriptions and performance data.
  • Backtest results must be based on real historical data and clearly labeled.
  • Live performance tracking should be enabled for transparency.
  • Strategies found to manipulate performance data will be removed and the publisher may be banned.
  • Paid strategies must deliver value consistent with their description and pricing.

4. Signal Broadcast Rules

  • Signals must include clear entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels.
  • Signal providers should maintain a public track record.
  • Do not broadcast signals for the purpose of market manipulation.
  • Signal providers are encouraged to provide reasoning for each signal.
  • Excessive false or misleading signals may result in removal from the platform.

5. Copy Trading Conduct

  • Leader traders must maintain transparent, verifiable performance records.
  • Leader traders should not guarantee returns or misrepresent their risk profile.
  • Followers should perform their own due diligence before copying a trader.
  • Both leaders and followers must understand that past performance does not guarantee future results.

6. Enforcement

We take community guidelines seriously. Violations may result in:

  • Warning: A formal notice for first-time or minor violations.
  • Content Removal: Deletion of offending posts, strategies, or signals.
  • Temporary Suspension: Restriction of community features for a set period.
  • Permanent Ban: Account termination for severe or repeated violations.
  • Legal Action: In cases of fraud, market manipulation, or illegal activities.

7. Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it through the in-app reporting feature or contact us at report@quantai.app. We review all reports and take appropriate action.

8. Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines periodically. Changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email. Continued use of community features after updates constitutes acceptance.
